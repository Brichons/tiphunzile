Grow your academic potential on Tiphunzile platform

Tiphunzile is a learning platform for secondary school pupils. The site is aimed at providing learners with the necessary materials that prepare them adequately for the final exams. We provide individualized performance accessement based on the acquired knowlegde and test the scholars' ability to tackle given tasks in a specified timeframe.
